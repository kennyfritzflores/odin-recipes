Nilagang Baboy

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Kg Pork Belly (liempo)
  • 1/2 Kg cabbage
  • 1/4 Kg potatoes
  • 1 tbs cracked pepper corns
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 big onions
  • 2 tbs salt or to taste/li>

    Steps:

      Step 1: Heat 1 L water to boil.
      Step 2: Put pork belly. Wait until boil.
      Step 3: After 10 minutes. Put pepper, garlic and onions.
      Step 4: After 5 minutes, add pepper and salt.
      Step 5: After 5 minutes, add potatoes.
      Step 6: After 5 minutes, add cabbage.
      Step 7: After 5 minutes. Nilaga is ready to serve.
