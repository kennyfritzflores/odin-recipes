Tokwa't Baboy

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 Kg Pork Face (maskara)
  • 1/4 Kg Tofu
  • 1/4 cup Cooking Oil
  • 1 tbs cracked pepper corns
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 2 medium size onions
  • 1/2 cups vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • salt to taste/li>

    Steps:

      Step 1: Fry tofu and dice by about 1/2 inch in size. Set aside.
      Step 2: Fry pork until crispy brown and dice by about 1/2 inch in size. Set aside.
      Step 3: Put vinegar and heat pan until boil.
      Step 4: Add juliened onions and garlic and pepper corns.
      Step 5: After 5 minutes, add soy sauce and salt to taste and wait until boil.
      Step 6: Mix the sauce, pork and tofu. Serve hot.
