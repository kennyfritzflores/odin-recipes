Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 1 Kg Chicken or Pork
  • 1/2 cup Cooking Oil
  • 5 pcs Bay leaves
  • 2 tbs cracked pepper corns
  • 1 head garlic
  • 2 big onions
  • 1 1/2 cups vinegar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbs salt or to taste/li>

    Steps:

      Step 1: Heat cooking pan to a medium heat.
      Step 2: Fry garlic. After 2 minutes fry onions.
      Step 3: Slowly stir fry chickec or pork.
      Step 4: After 10 minutes, add pepper and salt.
      Step 5: After 5 minutes, add vinegar.
      Step 6: After 5 minutes, add soy sauce.
      Step 7: After 10 minutes. The adobo is done.
